Thailand

Overview

Thailand is a Southeast Asian country known for its tropical beaches, rich cultural heritage, and strong expat communities. Popular relocation destinations include Bangkok, Chiang Mai, Phuket, and Pattaya.

  • Capital: Bangkok
  • Language: Thai (Thai script)
  • Currency: Thai Baht (THB)
  • Time zone: ICT (UTC+7)

Relocation Summary

Thailand offers several visa and residency routes suitable for tourists, students, workers, retirees, and investors (including the SMART visa). Healthcare in major cities is modern and affordable. Cost of living varies widely by city and lifestyle.

For full visa details and specific requirements see the dedicated visa page: Thailand — Visa & Entry.
