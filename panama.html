Global Hub: Panama

Panama is a modern, well‑connected country with attractive residency options. Below are core visa categories (sourced from Panama's Servicio Nacional de Migración and other official government pages), expat areas and practical notes.

Residency & Visa Pathways

Official source: Servicio Nacional de Migración — migracion.gob.pa

  • Pensionado (Retiree): Proof of lifetime pension income (commonly cited minimum ~$1,000 USD/month). Official guidance and benefits listed on Panama migration pages.
  • Friendly Nations / Residency (policy changes): Historically enabled fast residency for citizens of friendly nations via residence/investment; policies have evolved — check official migration guidance for current qualifying steps.
  • Investment / Professional routes: Investor-based residency options based on qualifying investments or company sponsorship.
  • Work & Employment — Employer-sponsored work permits and temporary resident options for qualified workers.
  • Digital Nomad / Remote Worker — New temporary visa types for remote workers have been announced and implemented; refer to official government pages for eligibility and income thresholds.
  • Permanent residency: Follows multi-year qualifying temporary residency or family ties; consult official regulations for timing and required documentation.

Popular Expat Cities & Regions

  • Panama City: Financial & healthcare hub, highest connectivity and services.
  • Boquete (Chiriquí Highlands): Mild climate, strong retiree community and outdoor activities.
  • Coronado / Pacific Beaches: Beach communities popular with expats, gated neighborhoods and resorts.

Healthcare, Internet & Practical Notes

  • Private hospitals and clinics in Panama City offer high-quality care; private insurance averages vary (est. $70–$220+/month depending on coverage & age).
  • Typical urban internet speeds often range 40–150 Mbps.
  • Territorial tax regime: Panama taxes Panama-sourced income; foreign-sourced income can be exempt in many cases — consult a tax professional and official sources.

Cost of Living Estimate (Couple)

$2,200 – $4,000 USD / month

  • High end: Panama City downtown living with private services & dining out.
  • Low end: Smaller towns or outside city core.
  • Note: Territorial tax rules can favor some foreign income.
