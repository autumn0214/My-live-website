Legal Frameworks

Common Central American Residency Visas

We specialize in these three highly popular visa categories for North American and European expats.

üí∞

Pensionado Visa

The Retiree Pathway

**Who it's for:** Retirees with guaranteed monthly income.

  • **Requirement:** Proof of a fixed, lifetime monthly income (usually minimum **$1,000 USD**).
  • **Benefit:** Allows the holder and dependents to live in the country.
  • **Key Countries:** Costa Rica, Panama.
üè¶

Rentista Visa

The Passive Income Pathway

**Who it's for:** Those with substantial savings but not yet retired.

  • **Requirement:** Must deposit a large sum (e.g., **$60,000 - $100,000 USD** in a local bank or prove equivalent monthly income for 2-5 years).
  • **Benefit:** Provides temporary residency with no minimum age requirement.
  • **Key Countries:** Costa Rica, Panama.
üå¥

Qualified Retired Person (QRP)

Belize's Expat Incentive

**Who it's for:** Retirees seeking significant tax breaks.

  • **Requirement:** Be over 45 and show **$2,000 USD** monthly income from outside Belize.
  • **Benefit:** **Tax-free income** from outside Belize and duty exemptions on household goods and a vehicle.
  • **Key Country:** Belize.
